# SCQA & Issue Tree — WhatsUp Logistics (Brazil Moto Fleet)

## SCQA
- Situation: WhatsUp Logistics runs a WhatsApp-first delivery marketplace with 1.8k active moto drivers across Brazil.
- Complication: Driver supply in São Paulo is flattening while SLA breaches on evening routes are rising; NPS dropped 7 points QoQ.
- Question: How do we restore SLA reliability in SP while scaling to new cities without eroding margins?
- Initial Answer/Hypothesis: Improve driver density and routing quality in SP through supply incentives and route optimization, then port the playbook to new cities.
