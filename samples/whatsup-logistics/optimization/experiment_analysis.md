## Summary
The proactive WhatsApp nudge treatment outperformed the control group, reducing SLA breaches and manual interventions without harming driver satisfaction.

## Insights
- Drivers appreciated proactive heads-up messages, especially in congested downtown areas.
- No statistically significant increase in driver churn or opt-outs observed.
- Proactive messages triggered quicker stall resolutions (median 6 minutes faster).
