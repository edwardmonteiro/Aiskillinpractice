# Optimization · Retrospective (São Paulo & Rio Pilot)

## What Went Well
- WhatsApp live tracking reduced dispatcher manual check-ins by 42%.
- Proactive nudges prevented 35 potential SLA breaches during first month.
- Driver onboarding sessions received positive feedback (4.6/5 satisfaction).

## What Didn't Go Well
- Belo Horizonte expansion delayed due to pending Meta API quota approval.
- Support documentation initially lacked Spanish translation for tourists.
- Some drivers experienced high data usage when streaming location continuously.

## Lessons Learned
- Provide clear quick reply training videos accessible via WhatsApp to reduce confusion.
- Implement adaptive GPS sampling based on motion to conserve battery and data.
- Maintain stronger alignment with finance to handle PIX exceptions proactively.
