# Definition · OKR Drafting

## Objective
Deliver a WhatsApp-native driver tracking experience that boosts delivery reliability and customer trust across Brazil.

## Key Results
1. Increase on-time delivery rate from 82% to **95%** in São Paulo and Rio by the end of Q2.
2. Reduce dispatcher manual check-ins per rider from 6/hour to **2/hour** by automating status pings.
3. Achieve **80%** customer satisfaction (CSAT) on WhatsApp status updates during pilot cities.
4. Launch PIX-based payout flow with < **24 hour** settlement for 95% of riders.

## Initiatives
- Implement live location sharing and stall detection features described in [`tech_spec.md`](../delivery/tech_spec.md).
- Build dispatcher monitoring dashboard (see [`dashboard_brief.md`](../delivery/dashboard_brief.md)).
- Localize WhatsApp templates and escalation scripts (coordinate with legal per [`skill_release.md`](../governance/skill_release.md)).
- Train support team on new workflows and update runbooks.
